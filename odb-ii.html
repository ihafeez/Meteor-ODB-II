ODB-II Codes

ODB-II is short for "On-board diagnostics, version 2" - it's the standard way way for vehicles to self-diagnose problems and emit DTCs - diagnostic trouble codes. It also allows for real-time monitoring of vehicle paramaters such as speed, fuel consumption, and emissions.

I use a ScanGuage tool like the one at the right on my car. There are also some less expensive models available.
