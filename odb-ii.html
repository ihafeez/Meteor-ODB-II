ODB-II Code Lookup

ODB-II is short for "On-board diagnostics, version 2" - it's the standard way way for vehicles to self-diagnose problems and emit DTCs - diagnostic trouble codes.

It also allows for real-time monitoring of vehicle paramaters such as speed, fuel consumption, and emissions.
